## Top 20 发布渠道

按覆盖面、独立开发者相关性、发布曝光能力精选。

1. [Product Hunt](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/product-hunt) - 产品发现平台，适合发布日集中曝光。
2. [Hacker News](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/hacker-news) - 技术用户密集，反馈直接。
3. [Indie Hackers](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/indie-hackers) - 独立开发者社区，适合讲故事和复盘。
4. [BetaList](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/betalist) - 适合获取早期用户。
5. [Peerlist](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/peerlist) - 开发者/创作者受众明显。
6. [DevHunt](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/devhunt) - 偏开发者工具曝光。
7. [Fazier](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/fazier) - 新产品目录站。
8. [Uneed](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/uneed) - 产品收录与发布曝光。
9. [Startup Stash](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/startup-stash) - 创业资源聚合。
10. [SaaSHub](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/saashub) - SaaS 发现与对比流量。
11. [AlternativeTo](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/alternativeto) - 替代品搜索意图强。
12. [Toolpilot](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/toolpilot) - 工具类目录受众垂直。
13. [Future Tools](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/future-tools) - AI/工具用户群体。
14. [There Is An AI For That](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/there-s-an-ai-for-that) - AI 用例流量入口。
15. [G2](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/g2) - B2B 软件口碑与可信度。
16. [Capterra](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/capterra) - B2B 采购意图明显。
17. [SourceForge](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/sourceforge) - 老牌软件目录站。
18. [SideProjectors](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/sideprojectors) - 侧重 side project 用户群。
19. [Launching Next](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/launching-next) - 新项目发布展示。
20. [Microlaunch](https://launchdirectories.com/directory/microlaunch) - 适合微型产品发布。

更多条目见：[docs/platforms/sites.md](docs/platforms/sites.md)。
